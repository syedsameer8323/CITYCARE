Priority Engine

Fairer, faster resolution

Traditional systems process complaints in the order they arrive. CityCare uses a weighted scoring formula so a chemical spill near a school is never stuck behind a minor littering report.

The score is recalculated automatically every time the admin panel loads — complaints that have been waiting longer gradually gain weight, preventing indefinite starvation.

Score = (severity × 0.50)
      + (wait_days × 0.30)
      + (area_load × 0.20)
